Welcome to the Pokies Club: Australia’s Top Spot for Poker Lovers

Explore the finest poker experiences at the Pokies Club. Whether you're just starting out or a veteran player, you'll discover the perfect setting to enhance your skills and enjoy thrilling gameplay.

Playing at the Pokies Club

The Pokies Club is located on the north eastern corner of level 1 at Hendon Hotel.

Pokies Club provides two options for playing poker: tournaments and cash games, also known as ring games. The main distinction between them is that players in cash games can leave at any time, while tournament participants must play until the end to be eligible for prizes.

TRADING HOURS:
Monday – Closed Tuesday 7pm – Late Wednesday 7pm – Late Thursday 7pm – Late Friday 5pm – Late Saturday 2pm – Late Sunday – Closed

Cash Game

Cash games utilize chips that can be exchanged for real money. Players can register for a cash game at Pokies Club starting thirty minutes before it opens. They have the option to join immediately or receive an email notification if they are on the waiting list.

No Limit Texas Hold’em:

  • Blinds 2/2
  • Buy in $50 – $300
  • Blinds 2/5
  • Buy in $150 – $800

Pot Limit Omaha:

  • Blinds 1/3
  • Buy in $100 – $300

Tournaments

No Limit Texas Hold’em tournaments are held at Pokies Club every Tuesday, with buy-ins beginning at $200. Players can register for all poker tournaments at Pokies Club Cashier, which is situated next to Pokies Club on the first level.

Get in to Hendon Hotel to play the biggest game in town with:

  • $10,000 guaranteed prize pool
  • $200 buy-in ($165 prize pool contribution. $35 Hendon Hotel fee)
  • 20-minute blind levels
  • $30,000 starting stack
  • $5,000 early bird bonus for players seated at the beginning of the first hand

Tournaments starts at 19:30  on Tuesdays, with late registration open until the start of the sixth level. Please note that tournaments are not held on public holidays.

$100 Freeze Out tournaments on Thursday nights:

  • Freeze Out
  • 100 buy-in ($80 prize pool contribution. $20 Hendon Hotel)
  • 15-minute blind levels
  • $20,000 starting stack

Tournaments starts at 19:30 on Tuesdays, with late registration open until the start of the sixth level. Please note that tournaments are not held on public holidays.
